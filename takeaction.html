Eatwell Guide

    Aim for at least 5 portions of fruit and veg a day
  • 1/3 of food should be starchy carbs such as wholewheat pasta, bread, rice, potatoes etc
  • 1/5 should be dairy such as milk or cheese which has calcium to keep our bones healthy.
    • Ty to go for lower fat and sugar products eg semi skimmed milk
  • Protein is very important in out diets for growth and repair. It is fund in meat, pulses, beans, lentils and eggs. Aim for at least 2 portions of fish a week with 1 being oily.
  • Fats should be eaten sparingly and you should make an effort to eat unsaturated fats like olive oil.
  • Sugar is not needed in our diet so we should only eat it as a treat. Children should have no more than 24g a day
  • It is recommended to drink 6-8 glasses of water a day





Exercise



Children aged 5-18 should aim for 60 mins of moderate intensity physical activity across the week. Moderate activity are activities that raise your heart rate and make you feel warmer. Examples include:

-Walking to school
-Cycling
-Riding a scooter
-Playing on the playground
-Skipping ropes
-Gymnastics
